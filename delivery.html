Shipping And Lead Time

Terms of production depends directly upon its volume. Despite volumes and complexity of order our company is able to proceed with any order within agreed terms. Terms of production as terms of delivery are indicated in both: purchase contract and proforma invoice in advance. Generally, we deliver our products by air, such method of delivery allows the goods to be delivered to any spot of the world (door–to-door) within several days or so. «Alumaproof Technologies» LLC works directly with such courier companies as: TNT, FeDex, DHL etc., which provide qualitative logistics and online tracking services. We understand how it is important for our clients to plan delivery time of products to their warehouse. Our colleagues are always ready to consult our clients concerning current order status and delivery.
